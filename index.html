About

Hello! This is a page about languages in Asia, presented in a map. I listed maximal 5 langauges per country(for the sake of space) by their ranking of popularity, but for some countries there are actually far more than 5.

Feel free to dig deeper into the languages, you might discover that 1 'little-knwon' language is speaking in 10 different countries, or 1 country has 3 official langauges!
